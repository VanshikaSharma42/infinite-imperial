About Us

For over 15 years, we have been the trusted partner for discovering investors seeking premium off-plan properties worldwide. Our commitment to excellence and deep industry knowledge sets us apart in the luxury real estate market.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Luxury Estates began with a simple vision: to connect international investors with the world's most prestigious off-plan properties. What started as a boutique consultancy has evolved into a global network spanning 25+ countries.

Our expertise lies in identifying emerging luxury markets and partnering with renowned developers who share our commitment to quality and innovation. We've facilitated over $2 billion in property investments, helping clients secure their dream homes and profitable investments.

Today, we continue to lead the industry with cutting-edge technology, personalized service, and an unwavering dedication to excellence that has earned us recognition as the premier destination for luxury off-plan properties.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional service and expert guidance in luxury off-plan property investments, ensuring our clients make informed decisions that exceed their expectations while building lasting relationships based on trust and integrity.
